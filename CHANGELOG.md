## [1.0.0] - 2025-01-XX

### Added

-   **Complete E-commerce Platform** with Laravel 12 backend
-   **Filament Admin Panel** with comprehensive management features
-   **Vue 3 + Inertia.js Frontend** with modern UI/UX
-   **Product Management System** with CRUD operations
-   **Category Management** with hierarchical organization
-   **Customer Management** with profiles and order history
-   **Order Processing System** with status tracking
-   **Shopping Cart** with persistent session management
-   **Responsive Design** optimized for all devices
-   **Search and Filtering** functionality
-   **Admin Dashboard** with analytics and reports
-   **Low Stock Alerts** for inventory management
-   **Sample Data** with realistic products and orders
-   **Authentication System** with Laravel Breeze
-   **Database Migrations** and seeders
-   **TailwindCSS Styling** with modern design
-   **Vite Asset Bundling** for optimal performance

### Features

-   **Product Management**: Full CRUD with image upload
-   **Category Management**: Organize products by categories
-   **Customer Management**: View and manage customer information
-   **Order Management**: Track orders with status updates
-   **Dashboard Analytics**: Revenue charts and order trends
-   **Inventory Tracking**: Low stock alerts and management
-   **Shopping Cart**: Add, update, and remove items
-   **Checkout Process**: Complete order placement
-   **Search & Filtering**: Advanced product search
-   **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach
-   **Admin Panel**: Professional admin interface
-   **Customer Portal**: User-friendly shopping experience

### Technical Stack

-   **Backend**: Laravel 12
-   **Admin Panel**: Filament 4
-   **Frontend**: Vue 3 + Inertia.js
-   **Styling**: TailwindCSS
-   **Database**: MySQL
-   **Build Tool**: Vite
-   **Authentication**: Laravel Breeze
